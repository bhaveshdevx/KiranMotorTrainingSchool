# About Kiran Motor Training School

## Our Story
Serving Devichapada and nearby areas since 2014, Kiran Motor Training School is dedicated to creating safe, confident, and responsible drivers. We believe in "Safety First" and provide comprehensive training that goes beyond just passing the driving test. Our mission is to empower individuals with the skills they need to navigate roads safely and efficiently.

## Our Services

### 1. Car Driving (Beginner Course)
* **Duration:** 20 Days
* **Price:** Starting from ₹5000
* **Description:** A complete training program designed for beginners. This course covers everything from basic car controls and traffic rules to parking techniques and confident on-road driving.

### 2. Refresher Course
* **Duration:** 7 Days
* **Price:** Starting from ₹1500
* **Description:** Tailored for license holders who may have lost touch with driving. This course focuses on building confidence, brushing up on skills, and getting you back behind the wheel comfortably.

### 3. Two-Wheeler Training
* **Duration:** 10 Days
* **Price:** Starting from ₹2000
* **Description:** Learn to ride a scooter or motorcycle safely. Our instructors focus on balance, control, and road safety rules for two-wheelers.
